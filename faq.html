Who is developing Elim?

• NBP Projects Limited, the company developing Elim Estate is made up of a group of vibrant, experienced, and forward thinking individuals who have a God given passion to deliver quality services in construction, Real Estate Development and related disciplines. This passion has led us delivering several turnkey projects in and around the country.

Can someone tell me how I can afford to purchase any of the houses at Elim Estate.

• Through the use of the National Housing Fund (NHF). This allows you to obtain a mortgage loan at 6% per annum which can be used to purchase any of the housing units.
